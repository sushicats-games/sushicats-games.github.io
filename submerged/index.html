Submerged

A subacvatic ecosystem exploration game.

Become part of an underwater ecosystem

As the player, you represent a part of the life that exists and continually evolves in this ecosystem.

Transforming Life

When eaten by a fish, you continue your journey as the fish.

When you die, you return into the soil and grow back as an algae.

Become the predator

Fish have mobility and they can explore the entire environment. They survive and grow by eating. Over time, well-fed fish will reproduce.

Eventually, all fish die of old age, but they can also die of hunger. Their well-being is indicated by their color, the better they are, the brighter their color. Near death they become almost white.

Death is not really the end but the beginning of a new life. Life is continuously transforming.
